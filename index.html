AI Evolution Simulation

This is a simulation I created that involves potentially hundreds of small neural network creatures that evolve their thinking over time, and it runs in your browser! The creatures are able to control their forward thrust and a degree of left/right rotation, and their goal is to consume food that is randomly generated in the world. If you run and observe for about five to ten minutes, you should see families of creatures specifically designed to maximize their speed and eventually move in wave-like patterns. Take a look!
